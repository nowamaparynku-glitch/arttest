About

Laura Gizicka is a Polish sculptor and illustrator whose work inhabits the borderland between myth and flesh. Working primarily in ceramic — from raw stoneware to delicate porcelain — she sculpts organic, feminine forms that carry the weight of ancient archetypes.

Her illustrations extend this mythology into vibrant digital realms, drawing on Slavic symbolism, sacred geometry, and the raw energy of the natural world.

Her work has been shown at galleries and art fairs across Poland. She lives and works between studio and fire.
